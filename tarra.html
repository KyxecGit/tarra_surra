Our Story

Where Tradition Meets Artistry

Nestled in the vibrant heart of Bangkok, Tarra is more than a restaurant — it is an invitation to experience Thailand's rich culinary heritage reimagined through the lens of modern fine dining. Every element, from the carefully curated interiors to the meticulously crafted dishes, speaks to our commitment to excellence.

Founded in 2019 by Chef Namo and a team of visionary restaurateurs, Tarra has quickly become one of Bangkok's most sought-after dining destinations, earning recognition from the world's leading culinary publications and discerning guests alike.

The Visionary

Chef Namo

Executive Chef & Culinary Director

Born in Bangkok and raised in Sakaeo, a quiet province in eastern Thailand, Chef Namo’s earliest memories are of standing beside his grandmother and father in the kitchen. What began as playful curiosity slowly became a meaningful daily ritual — cooking as an expression of care, gratitude, and connection.

His philosophy of Thai cuisine is rooted in three essential qualities: flavor, variety, and shareability. At its heart is balance — the thoughtful harmony of acidity, sweetness, saltiness, bitterness, and fattiness. Through Tarra, he creates a space where tradition is respected, flavors are balanced with intention, and every meal celebrates Thai culture with sincerity and warmth.
